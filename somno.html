Somno AI

independent research project on sleep optimisation — modelling recovery patterns based on the level of sleep deprivation, without needing sensors or expensive hardware.

  • custom hybrid convolutional neural networks combined with probabilistic models
  • neural architecture optimised with neural architecture search and random search
  • free, non-excludable application anyone can use
  • accepted to IEEE ACDSA Conference with an oral presentation
  • presented at the S.T. Yau competition in Hong Kong to Ivy League professors
  • published in IEEE Xplore as solo first author
