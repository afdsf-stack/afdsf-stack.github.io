pedestrian safety app

patent-pending background application to prevent severe injuries caused by phone usage while walking — with the potential to reduce hundreds of thousands of texting-while-walking accidents.

  • MobileNet trained on an expansive dataset for recognising dangerous objects
  • eye-tracking library to detect whether a person's eyes are focused on their phone screen
  • VotingClassifier on the Human Activity Recognition dataset — 84.8% accuracy using only 0.055% of sensor headers
  • Flutter frontend communicating with a Flask server backend
